About TimeWell

Discover the story behind TimeWell and how we’re helping people worldwide organize their lives and achieve their goals.

Our Story

TimeWell was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to help people take control of their time and live more productive, fulfilling lives. Our founders, a group of productivity enthusiasts and tech innovators, noticed a common problem—people were overwhelmed by their daily tasks, struggling to balance work, personal goals, and life’s many demands. They set out to create a solution that would simplify task management, goal setting, and time tracking, all in one intuitive platform.

From a small startup in San Francisco, TimeWell has grown into a global platform, serving over 50,000 users across 100+ countries. We’ve helped professionals, students, entrepreneurs, and families organize their lives, achieve their dreams, and find more time for what truly matters. Our journey is just beginning, and we’re committed to continuously improving our tools to meet the evolving needs of our community.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

At TimeWell, our mission is to empower individuals and teams to manage their time effectively, achieve their goals, and lead balanced lives. We believe that with the right tools, anyone can unlock their full potential and make every moment count.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone has the tools and knowledge to master their schedules, pursue their passions, and live with purpose. TimeWell aims to be the leading platform for productivity, inspiring millions to take control of their time and create a better future.
